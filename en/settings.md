# Settings overview

Open **Settings** from the dock (gear icon). Tabs across the top:

1. **Appearance**  
2. **Notifications**  
3. **Account**  
4. **Billing**  
5. **Privacy & Data**  
6. **Protection**  
7. **Adblocker**  
8. **Proxy**  
9. **Task Manager**  

## Appearance

- **Language** — UI language (English, Português, Français, Español, Deutsch, Italiano, Русский).
- **Interface Theme** — **Dark** or **Light** (labels in the selector are `Dark` / `Light`; marketing names in translations may differ).
- **Animations** — Smooth transitions on or off.

## Notifications

See [Notifications](notifications.md).

## Account

Magic link, device label, disconnect, **Cloud Sync** toggles, push/pull, and the **App Updates** card (check for updates, restart to install). See [Account & cloud sync](account-and-cloud-sync.md) and [Installation](installation.md) for updates behavior.

## Billing

Shows current plan and renewal when subscribed; **Manage Plan** / **Check Plan** when available. See [Plans & billing](plans-and-billing.md).

## Privacy & Data, Protection, Adblocker, Proxy, Task Manager

Each has a dedicated doc page in this folder.

## Tray behavior (Windows & Linux)

On **Windows** and **Linux**, weballoon can show a **tray icon**. **Click** the icon to reopen the window; **right‑click** for **Open weballoon** and **Quit**. Closing the main window may **hide** to the tray instead of quitting, depending on defaults—use **Quit** from the tray when you want to fully exit.

**macOS** does not use this tray pattern in the current build.
