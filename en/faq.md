# FAQ

## Does weballoon replace my normal browser?

It complements it. weballoon is best for **stable sets** of web apps you treat like desktop tools, with **isolation** and **workspace** grouping.

## Do workspaces share cookies?

**No.** Isolation is **per app**, not shared across a workspace. Workspace is only **organization**.

## What syncs to the cloud?

Your **apps and workspaces configuration** when Cloud Sync is on—not your site passwords or browsing contents, per the in-app description. See [Account & cloud sync](account-and-cloud-sync.md).

## Can I use two accounts on Gmail / Slack / etc.?

Yes, by adding **two separate apps** (different names, same or different URLs where the service allows). You cannot install the **same URL twice in one workspace**; use two workspaces or different entry URLs.

## Is there a mobile app?

This documentation covers the **desktop** product only.

## Does weballoon work offline?

Only where the **underlying website** works offline. weballoon does not turn arbitrary sites into offline-first apps.

## Where is my data?

**Settings → Privacy & Data** shows the path and can **open the folder**. Data is **local-first**; cloud pieces are optional configuration sync and billing state.

## How much does Pro cost?

Open **Plans** in the app; prices are shown live there (currency and amounts can change).

## Is there a “Team” plan in the app?

The translation files mention team-oriented **feature labels** for billing UIs, but the **built-in plan list** in the codebase is **Free**, **Pro**, and **Pro Annual**. Trust whatever plans **Plans** actually lists for your account.
