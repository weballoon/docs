# Plans & billing

Open **Plans** from the dock (star icon) to see **Billing & Plans**.

## Free plan

The built-in plan list describes **Free** as including:

- **10 apps**
- **2 workspaces**
- **Isolated sessions**

Enforcement in the app:

- You cannot create more than **10** apps.
- You cannot create more than **2** named workspaces.
- If you hit the app limit, **My Apps** shows a banner with an **Upgrade** action.

## Pro and Pro Annual

**Pro** and **Pro Annual** list **unlimited apps**, **unlimited workspaces**, and **priority support** in the product copy.

Upgrade requires a **linked email** on this device. If you are not linked, the Plans page explains that you should link in **Settings** first. If a magic link is pending, it asks you to finish email verification before paying.

## Managing a subscription

When the account is connected and the backend allows it, you may see **Manage Plan** (opens the provider’s customer portal) and **Check Plan** to refresh status on this device.

Exact prices and currency are shown on the Plans page in the app (they can change; trust the live screen).

## After purchase

When the device is linked, the app notes that the subscription **syncs automatically** to this device after checkout.
