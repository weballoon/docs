# Adblocker

Open **Settings → Adblocker**.

## Global switch

**Enable Global Adblocker** turns network-level blocking **on or off** for all apps that respect the setting.

When blocking is off, a **warning** banner explains that trackers and ads will be visible again.

## Per-app override

Under **App Specific Controls**, you can **disable** blocking for individual apps if a site breaks or loads incorrectly with the blocker on.

Per-app rows are grouped by workspace like other settings panels.

## After changing settings

The app notes that adblocker changes may need an **app restart or refresh** to fully apply to **already open** windows.

## What it is not

weballoon does not promise every ad format is blocked; heavy sites may still show first-party content that looks like promotion. The goal stated in the app is fewer ads and trackers, better performance, and better privacy **when** the blocker is enabled.
